# &cong; Congruent
A collaborative review process to maintain standards of quality, improve performance, and promote oringinal intent.

A **congruent** paper is not considered an original work, but instead an **interpreted work** of rules, regulations, etc. This interpreted work is the author's Draft. The author must find a peer to review thier works and collaborate the paper into an Incongruent version. This Incongruent paper is submitted and remains incongruent until reviewed by another peer in collaboration with the Author and Incongruent Author. If the document survives the scrutiny it becomes Congruent.
